Gameplay

Stack falling letter blocks, create meaningful words, and clear the board before it fills up.

  • Letter blocks fall from the top of the board.
  • Move blocks left, right, or drop them quickly.
  • When letters form a valid word (left-to-right or top-to-bottom), they turn yellow and clear.
  • Words must meet the minimum root length to be accepted, rewarding smart placement.
  • Survive as long as you can and chase the highest score.

Skill tools

Use powerful skills in critical moments:

  • Joker – wildcard letter
  • Shuffle – shuffle the board
  • Remove Letter – clear a single block
  • Change Letter – transform a letter

Privacy & Data

Alphuzzle is built to be privacy-friendly and simple.

What we collect

The game itself does not collect, store, or process any personal data. There are no accounts, avatars, nicknames, age prompts, or user profiles in Alphuzzle.

Third-party advertising networks may use device-level identifiers to show ads. We do not see or store any of this information.
